Tzatziki

Description

Dope ass greek dish that is full of greek yogurt, cucumber, garlic. Florence Pugh makes it best.

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt
  • Cucumber
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop up the cucumber
  2. Let the cucumber drain. Add salt, then add it to a bowl lined with a paper towel
  3. Finely mince garlic
  4. Mix all together and serve
