Biryani

Description

Dope ass indian dish that is full of chicken, rice, spices, and veggies. Mama Bhardwaj makes it the best.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Salt, Pepper, Cardamon, Cinnamon, Turmeric, Garam Masala
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Cook the chicke
  2. Boil the rice
  3. For extra flavor, take a portion of the spice mixture meant for the chicken and add it to the rice while cooking
  4. Serve
